Zair Azgur

Here's a time line of Zair Azgur's life:

  • 1908 - born in v. Molchany, Belarus
  • 1925 - graduated Institute in Vitebsk
  • 1942 - 1945 - during war worked in patriotic movement in Moscow
  • 1944 - became national painter of Belarus. Worked as main painter of Goverment House.
  • 1945 - got married on Galina Gorelovoy
  • 1972 - created sculptures on Yakub Kolas Square
  • 1995 - dies at the age of 87.
